RULES

"Welcome to DoubleDown! The goal of the game is to beat the computer by rolling specific dice combinations. If you win the round by rolling the combination first you can choose to double down and try to double the round score. If you win the double down, your round score will double and we will move to the next round. However, if you lose the double down round, the opponent will get the double score for that round. Whoever has more points after round six will have an advantage when playing Pig to win the game. Good luck!"
